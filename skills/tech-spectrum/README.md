# Tech Spectrum — AI Disruption Positioning

> Part of the Clawsight Scene Skill ecosystem

## What It Does

Tech Spectrum places you on the AI transformation map. By crossing your personal profile with AI industry trends, it shows where you stand, what's coming, and where your unique opportunities lie.

**It answers**: "In the AI revolution, where exactly do I stand? What's my unique opportunity?"

## The Five-Level AI Spectrum

```
AI-vulnerable → AI-adjacent → AI-augmented → AI-native → AI-shaping
```

Unlike binary "will AI replace me?" assessments, this spectrum gives a precise position with a clear upgrade path.

## Key Features

- **AI Spectrum Positioning**: Five-dimension assessment of your position
- **AI Exposure Analysis**: How AI impacts your specific industry and role
- **Trend × Profile Intersection**: Maps 8 major AI tracks against your profile to find convergence points, divergence risks, and opportunity windows
- **AI Anxiety Antidote**: Evidence-based framing that transforms anxiety into action
